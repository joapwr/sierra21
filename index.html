Common questions
Straight answers. No fluff.
How much does a build cost?
It depends on scope. Micro builds (single pages, quick tools) from £250. Websites and MVPs from £500. Full SaaS platforms and complex builds from £5,000+. You'll get a fixed price upfront — no hourly billing, no scope creep invoices. Rescue jobs and advisory sessions are scoped on inspection.
How fast can you deliver?
Working demos in 5–7 days for most projects. Small builds and prototypes often ship in 48–72 hours. We move at a pace most agencies can't match because there's no committee, no layers, no sign-off chain.
What if I just have a rough idea?
That's fine — most clients do. Submit what you have through the smart form. If the idea has potential, we'll come back with a clear scope and architecture. If it needs refining, we'll tell you that too.
Do I own the code?
Yes. 100%. Every line of code, every design asset, every deployment config. It's your product. You get full access to the repository on completion.
What about support after launch?
You get full ownership of the code and deployment. We hand over with documentation so you or your team can maintain it. If something breaks down the line, that's what our Rescue service is for — we'll take a look, scope the fix, and get it sorted.
How do we communicate?
Email and async messaging. No Zoom calls, no weekly standups. You'll get regular progress updates and working previews you can test yourself. If we need something from you, we ask once and clearly.
